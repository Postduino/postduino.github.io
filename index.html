What made you want to setup Postduino?

We love all things Arduino, and want to make it easy for others to join in. Cheap parts from China drop the barrier to entry, and mean we can tinker with new project ideas without the risk of ruining expensive components.

Can I join in without subscribing?

Absolutely! We make subscribing easy and cheap so you know the parts you need are going to be in your bucket of bits. If you already have the parts we're using, or would just prefer to buy them yourself then go for it!

How do you take payments for Postduino?

We want to spend as little time as possible handling money, or worrying about business things, so all payments are handled by PayPal. Orders are sent directly to parts sellers, and shipped directly to you.
